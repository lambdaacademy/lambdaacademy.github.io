Chat with our bots and other attendees

First you need to join network IoT_lambda_2_4 or IoT_lambda_5_0 (the password is lambdadays).

In the right bottom corner tap Toggle chat.



Username and password is constructed by joining all segments of your name from Lambda Days badge with dots. Remember that all characters with accents are converted to their ASCII counterparts, all non-alphanumeric characters are removed and all characters are lowercased. For example, if James Bond attended Lambda Days, his badge would look like this:



James would then use james.007.bond as a username and password to join a chat.



Once logged in, you can tap on Contacts tab to invite other attendees to conversation. Tap on Add a contact and type in someone else's JID (i.e. username constructed with previous rule followed by @xmpp.lambdadays.org).

Chatting with bots

Note: to see an effect of conversation with bots, you must be near Erlang Solutions stand.

Tap on the Rooms tab. Click Show rooms and join music_room. If the rooms list is empty, manually enter room's name and tap Join room. After joining type help and the bot will tell you what it can do for you!
